The Kek

The kek are a new race in Faerun, having appeared only about six months ago. No one knows where they come from or why they've suddenly arrived in Faerun, and no one knows their numbers since they are scattered around the continent. In any single location, only a few ever appeared, but they have since started to gather in settlements. There are only a few such settlements and none are big by human standards. One of the largest known settlements is in Shadowdale, with about 100 individuals, including all men, women, and children.

Standing about 3 1/2 feet tall and weight about 110 pounds, the kek have a reptillian appearance. They have hairless, leather-like skin of green and brown hues. Their bodies are completely hairless. Both males and female will usually wear only a loin cloth, but more recently females have started wearing small tops to draw less attention from non-kek.

The kek have settled in Shadowdale with the permission of Lord Mourngrym. They have built two large communal houses (shacks, really) south of the Toad Knoll straddling the western road leading in to Shadowdale, and they have built a large farm. So far, the Kek remain as poor as refugees typically are, but there are some signs that the farm may produce a good harvest. Unfortunately the Kek have been hampered in their efforts as some townsfolk have sabotaged their efforts.

Although the kek were given permission to settle by the Lord of Shadowdale, the local populace has not welcomed them with open arms. They are almost universally disdained, though most residents choose to keep them at arm's length. Some, however, are not content to simply ignore them and instead take every opportunity to harrass them. This is not helped by the fact that a few kek have taken to begging around Shadowdale, and a few others have taken to thievery. Or at least that is the claim of some locals when something goes missing, though no kek has ever been arrested for thievery. The kek, being of small size, have so far not responded to any such bigotry or attacks.

Combat: Although there are many rumors concerning the powers and combat prowess of the kek, none have been confirmed. Indeed, all verified reports suggest that the kek are a pacifistic race.

Habitat/Society: The specifics of kek society are not known to outsiders, but observers have commented that the kek seem to eschew traditional family organization in favor of larger communal living. The kek settlement has two larger structures that house numerous male and female kek, instead of smaller family dwellings that most other races prefer. There also does not appear to be any clear hierarchy among the adult kek.

The kek are excellent farmers and have been able to cultivate land near Toad Knoll that others have failed with. They mostly grow cassava, a staple of the kek diet, but they have tried several other cash crops. Most recently they have found success with tobacco, growing their own strain that has been growing in populararity throughout the Dalelands.

Ecology: The kek chose their location because they love the taste of toads and frogs, so the increased population of toads and frogs caused by the intermittent natural spring on Toad Knoll gives them a great hunting ground for one of their favorite foods. The kek also eat raw cassava, an uncommon vegetable in Faerun that if eaten unprocessed is poisonous to most other humanoids. It does not appear to bother the kek, however, and is in fact a staple of their diet. Otherwise, the kek diet is filled with the same sorts of foods that most humans eat.

The kek speak Common, but use a dialect that is very different than the one used in Faerun. As a result, they tend to have trouble communicating with others outside of their race. This has led to a general perception that the kek are of low intelligence, although whether or not this is true has not been proven.
